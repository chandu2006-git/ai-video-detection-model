      Under the Hood

      A two-stage architecture, built for time-aware detection

      A convolutional encoder extracts spatial artifacts from individual frames — texture irregularities, blending seams, unnatural lighting. A recurrent LSTM head then reads the resulting sequence to catch temporal inconsistencies that a single-frame classifier would miss: flicker, identity drift, and unstable motion.

      CNN Spatial Feature Extractor Per-frame artifact & texture analysis
      LSTM Temporal Sequence Reader Cross-frame consistency tracking
